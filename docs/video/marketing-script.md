# SmartCommission — Marketing Video Script

Version: 1.0
Duration: ~120 seconds
Last updated: 2026-06-18

---

SCENE 1 — HOOK (0s–10s)
Visual: Dark background. SmartCommission logo animates in with particle trails suggesting calculations cascading like data flow.
Narration: "Sales commission errors cost businesses millions — and destroy rep trust."
Text overlay: SmartCommission
Transition: Fade to bright UI

SCENE 2 — PROBLEM (10s–25s)
Visual: Split screen — left: frustrated rep staring at spreadsheet with RED #REF errors; right: clean SmartCommission dashboard.
Narration: "Manual spreadsheets. Disputed payouts. Late month-end calculations. Your reps deserve better — and so do you."
Text overlay: "There's a smarter way."
Transition: Swipe right

SCENE 3 — PRODUCT INTRO (25s–40s)
Visual: Dashboard overview. Attainment percentages tick up, earnings totals animate, status badges flip Pending → Approved in real time.
Narration: "Introducing SmartCommission — automated incentive compensation that your entire sales team can trust."
Text overlay: SmartCommission — Incentive Compensation, Automated.
Transition: Slide down

SCENE 4 — PLAN BUILDER (40s–58s)
Visual: Admin building a tiered commission plan. Rule blocks slide in — FLAT_RATE, then ACCELERATOR above 100% quota. Projected earnings curve updates live.
Narration: "Build any plan — flat rates, tiered progressives, accelerators, SPIFs — with a visual rule builder. No formulas, no errors."
Text overlay: Visual Plan Builder
Transition: Cut

SCENE 5 — REAL-TIME CALCULATIONS (58s–78s)
Visual: Deal syncs from Salesforce. Calculation engine animates — rules fire, earnings record created. Rep portal shows updated earnings instantly.
Narration: "Every deal triggers instant, auditable calculations. Reps see their earnings in real time — no more waiting for month-end."
Text overlay: Real-Time, Auditable Calculations
Transition: Cut

SCENE 6 — REP PORTAL & DISPUTES (78s–95s)
Visual: Rep portal on mobile. Attainment gauge at 87%. Rep raises a dispute — status changes Under Review → Resolved. Notification arrives.
Narration: "A self-service portal for every rep — attainment tracking, statements, and built-in dispute resolution that closes in days."
Text overlay: Rep Portal & Dispute Management
Transition: Zoom out

SCENE 7 — SOCIAL PROOF (95s–108s)
Visual: Stat counters: "Error rate < 0.01%", "Avg dispute resolution: 2.3 days", "Calculation time: < 1s per deal"
Narration: "Accurate to the cent. Fast enough for any team size. Trusted by finance leaders worldwide."
Text overlay: Accurate. Fast. Trusted.
Transition: Fade to CTA

SCENE 8 — CALL TO ACTION (108s–120s)
Visual: Clean landing page. SmartCommission logo centred. Indigo CTA button glows.
Narration: "Start your free trial today. No credit card required."
Text overlay: smartcommission.app  |  Start Free →
Transition: Fade out

## Production notes
- Colour palette: indigo-600 accent, slate-900 background, white cards
- Font: Geist
- Music: Upbeat modern instrumental, 128 BPM, no vocals
- Voice: Clear professional neutral accent
- Subtitles: White text, dark semi-transparent background, always on
- Formats: 16:9 (1920×1080) primary + 9:16 (1080×1920) for social
- Logo: bottom-right watermark throughout
