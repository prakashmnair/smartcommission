# SmartCommission — Marketing Video Script

Last updated: 2026-06-22
Version: 2.0
Duration: 30 seconds (primary) · Optional 2-min deep-dive from same storyboard

---

## Scene 1 — Hook / Problem (0–8s)

**Visual:** Frustrated sales rep staring at a spreadsheet with RED #REF errors. Month-end chaos — emails flying, disputes unresolved.

**Voiceover:**
> "Sales commission errors cost businesses millions — and destroy rep trust."

**Text on screen:** `SmartCommission`

**Transition:** Hard cut to clean dashboard.

---

## Scene 2 — Product + Hero Feature (8–22s)

**Visual:** Dashboard reveal — attainment percentages tick up, earnings totals animate, status badges flip Pending → Approved. Zoom into deal sync: a deal closes in CRM, SmartCommission instantly calculates the payout. Rep portal on mobile shows updated earnings in real time.

**Voiceover:**
> "SmartCommission automates every commission calculation. Every deal triggers an instant, auditable payout — and reps see their earnings in real time. Build any plan with a visual rule builder: flat rates, tiers, accelerators, SPIFs — no formulas, no errors."

**Text on screen:** `Incentive Compensation, Automated.`

**Transition:** Cut to stat card.

---

## Scene 3 — Social Proof (22–26s)

**Visual:** Three stat cards on clean dark background counting up.

**Voiceover:**
> "Error rate under 0.01%. Average dispute resolved in 2.3 days. Trusted by finance leaders worldwide."

**Text on screen:** `Error rate < 0.01%  ·  Disputes closed in 2.3 days`

**Transition:** Fade to CTA.

---

## Scene 4 — Call to Action (26–30s)

**Visual:** Clean landing page. Indigo CTA button glows. SmartCommission logo centred.

**Voiceover:**

## Production Notes

- Colour palette: indigo-600 accent, slate-900 background, white cards
- Font: Geist
- Music: Upbeat modern instrumental, 128 BPM, no vocals
- Voice: Clear professional neutral accent
- Subtitles: white text, dark semi-transparent background, always on
- Formats: 16:9 (1920×1080) primary + 9:16 (1080×1920) for social
- Logo: bottom-right watermark throughout
- AI video tools: Runway, Sora, HeyGen, Synthesia
