## Production Notes

- **Format:** 16:9 (1920×1080) primary. Export 9:16 (1080×1920) for LinkedIn/Instagram Reels as secondary.
- **Duration:** 120 seconds exactly for paid social. Cut a 30s highlight version using Scenes 1, 3, 6, 8.
- **Subtitles:** Burned-in white subtitles with black outline on all versions. Required for accessibility and auto-play (muted) environments.
- **Tools:** Structured for AI video production via Runway Gen-3, HeyGen avatar narration, or Sora. Alternatively: Figma → After Effects + screen recordings.
- **Music:** Royalty-free from Artlist or Musicbed. Genre: modern tech SaaS (Notion, Linear style).
- **Status:** Script approved. Storyboard draft. Production: pending.
